IB: International Baccalaureate

Study in English and prepare for studies abroad with IB If you're looking for a high school program that is recognized worldwide, the IB Diploma Programme at NTI Skövde is a great option to consider.

Study in English This programme teaches all of its subjects in English, so you improve your language skills while learning about a wide range of subjects. In your first year, PreDP (Pre-Diploma Programme), you will study 900p within the Swedish curriculum, with all subjects taught in English. The PreDP curriculum is designed to prepare you for success in the IB Diploma Programme, which you will begin in year 2 and 3.

Become part of a global community One of the best things about IB is that you will be part of a global community, which means you will meet classmates from all over the world. The education you receive through the IB Diploma Programme is designed to maintain high quality, ensuring that you receive a world-class education(öppnas i nytt fönster) that prepares you for global opportunities and challenges.

Choose from six subject groups The IB Diploma Programme offers six subject groups from which to choose six diploma subjects that match your interests and academic goals for further studies both in Sweden and abroad. Read more about this here.

Your chosen combination of subjects will shape your academic path and determine your eligibility for university studies, both in Sweden and abroad. Learn more about Designing Your Diploma(öppnas i nytt fönster) and the Diploma Circle to explore how to tailor your studies.

Examination subjects At NTI Gymnasiet Skövde, four of the examination subjects offered are taught at only a handful of IB schools in Sweden. These subjects are:

Computer Science (SL/HL) Visual Arts (SL/HL) Mathematics (HL) Japanese Ab Initio (SL). Discover why IB might be the perfect choice for you by exploring Why IB at NTI(öppnas i nytt fönster) and IB Facts & Figures(öppnas i nytt fönster).
